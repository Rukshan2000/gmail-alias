Gmail aliases are alternative email addresses you can create using your main Gmail account, without registering new accounts. They help you organize, filter, and protect your inbox.

  • + Aliases: Add "+keyword" after your username (e.g., yourname+shopping@gmail.com) to track signups or filter emails.
  • Dot Variants: Gmail ignores dots in usernames (your.name@gmail.com = yourname@gmail.com).
  • Random Aliases: Generate unique addresses for privacy or temporary use.

Uses: Manage newsletters, signups, work, shopping, and protect your main address from spam. Easily filter or search emails by alias.
